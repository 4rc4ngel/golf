About us

Early in March 2013 John Farrow and myself met and played a round of golf at my then club, Romford. We had recently met at TLC golf days and discussed the idea of a regular Tuesday meet, to accompany the monthly society days.

The call was then put out on our Twitter accounts to see if there was any interest among other members of TLC that were Essex based. A couple of weeks later we had a tee booked for 8am on a Tuesday at Mardyke Valley Golf Course. Six people showed up, Chris Allen, Dave Clark, John Farrow, Jon Brewster, Mike Inns, and myself. So that began our regular Tuesday meet!!!

Numbers grew quickly and soon topped the 12-16 we were originally thinking of. We moved around a little too, Stapleford Abbotts and Woolston Manor being the other courses played, then John struck a deal with the secretary of Woolston and that became our home for the remainder of the year.

A conversation with a friend of mine who lives in Twickenham about league formats in golf resulted in us copying his society although tweaking it slightly. So on June 18th 2013 with a merry band of 20 golfers we lined up at Woolston Manor to play the first event of our Summer Competition. We ran until October 15th when Lee Spackman became the first ever champion of Tuesday Club.

Our parting words were often “See you next Tuesday” and as needed a name for our group that felt as good as any. So the CUNextTuesday’s were born!! After that we stayed with Woolston and evolved our tournament from April to October finishing with what has become our traditional end of season night out.
